Lasagana


Description :

Based on the traditional lasagna alla Bolognese from the Emilia-Romagna province in Italy,
I grabbed what I loved most about the timeless, classic recipe and simplified it to make it
weeknight-friendly, and a new Delish favorite was born. There’s absolutely no way we can fully
express just how passionate we are about this lasagna, though one of our coworkers breaking his
self-imposed no-carbs rule to try a bite (and confess he’d marry anyone who made it for him)
might give you an idea. This lasagna can be eaten for any occasion—family Sunday dinner,
lunch with friends over a pitcher of sangria, or cold straight from the fridge after a night out
(it makes great leftovers).

Ingredients :

  • Cheese filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach

Steps :

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake
